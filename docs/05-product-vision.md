# Product Vision

Kourai is intended to become a workflow layer for marketing teams that need AI assistance without giving up control over brand voice, approvals, analytics, or account context.

The product direction is not just content generation. Kourai is being shaped as a system where teams can ask questions, retrieve relevant context, generate strategy or draft options, review risk, inspect historical performance, and keep human approval in the loop.

## Direction

- Chat-based control for local workflows and reports
- Modular account, campaign, product, and analytics concepts
- Learned and effective voice profiles for context-specific generation
- Historical-content classification and performance-aware learning
- X/Twitter as the first platform test case
- Additional platforms in later phases
- Optional controlled automation only after explicit authorization

## Product Principle

AI should make marketing workflows faster and more informed, but users should control publishing, risk tolerance, and brand direction.
